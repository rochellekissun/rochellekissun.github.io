My Sports Journey

2016: The Beginning

Developed a passion for soccer, inspired by Cristiano Ronaldo & Lionel Messi. Faced early struggles with injuries and criticism but committed to improving. Started strength and conditioning training to enhance performance.

2017: Dedication & Coaching

Focused on intense training to surpass high school teammates. Played multiple positions but excelled as a goalkeeper, finishing the season undefeated. Took on assistant coaching roles for both the school and senior teams.

2018: Club Soccer & Challenges

Played for St Clair United SC as a midfielder but preferred goalkeeping. Suffered a shoulder injury but continued playing for the high school team, leading to a strong season.

2019: Fresh Start & High School Finale

Joined Penrith FC, had a great season, and reached the grand finals (lost 1-0 to Glenmore Park FC). Faced setbacks but used the experience as motivation. Played the final season with the high school team before graduating.

2020-2023: Coaching & Further Development

Dedicated time to coaching and training at local soccer clubs. In 2021, coached Blacktown Workers FC U16 Girls D1 team, reaching near-finals before COVID lockdown. Studied sports coaching & management to further career development.

2024: The Comeback & New Challenges

Rejoined St Marys FC as a goalkeeper, leading the team to a 3-0 grand final victory. Stepped out of comfort zone and joined Nepean Ducks Gridiron Club as a wide receiver, enjoying the challenge of learning a new sport.

My Sports Journey

2016-2020

Played for St. Marys FC in various competitions, achieving multiple accolades and improving my skills both individually and as part of a team. My role involved leadership on and off the field, and I developed a strong bond with my teammates.

2021

Joined the Nepean Ducks as a wide receiver, despite limited prior experience. I quickly adapted to gridiron and participated in my first competitive match against the UC Stars in Canberra, marking a significant milestone in my sports journey.

2022-Present

Continued my gridiron career with the Nepean Ducks and now actively train and develop my skills for future seasons, with a focus on improving speed, tactics, and team collaboration.

2025 Goals

My goal is to win a gold medal with my teammates at St. Marys FC in 2025, as well as rejoin the Nepean Ducks and continue my growth in gridiron. I'm also working towards improving my leadership skills in both sports and personal development.

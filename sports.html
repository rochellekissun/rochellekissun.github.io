My Sports Journey

2016

Started playing soccer after developing a huge interest in the sport, inspired by my idols Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi. Although I faced challenges and injuries, I pushed through and trained hard, focusing on strength and conditioning.

2017

Spent a whole year training hard to outperform my teammates. Played various positions but enjoyed being a goalkeeper. I also discovered my passion for coaching and assisted in coaching my team and the senior team.

2018

Played as a midfielder for St Clair United SC, though I also performed well as a goalkeeper. Unfortunately, I suffered a shoulder injury but continued playing for the high school team, which had a fantastic season.

2019

Joined Penrith FC for a fresh start, and we had an excellent season. We reached the finals but lost 1-0 to Glenmore Park FC. It was also my final year with the high school team. Graduating was bittersweet, but I promised to return to win the grand final one day.

2020-2023

Post-graduation, I spent time training and coaching at local clubs. In 2021, I coached at Blacktown Workers FC with the U16 girls D1 team. Despite the pandemic, I continued studying coaching and sports team management.

2024

Rejoined St Marys FC as a goalkeeper for the AAW D5 team and led them to victory in the grand final, winning 3-0. I also ventured into gridiron with Nepean Ducks as a wide receiver, despite not knowing the sport well, but enjoyed the challenge.

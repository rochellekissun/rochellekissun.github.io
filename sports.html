My Sports Journey

2016: The Beginning

Fell in love with soccer, inspired by Ronaldo & Messi. Trained hard despite injuries and early struggles.

2017: Dedication & Coaching

Intense training led to success as a goalkeeper. Discovered coaching and assisted in training teams.

2018: Club Soccer & Challenges

Played for St Clair United SC as a midfielder and goalkeeper. Suffered a shoulder injury but continued playing.

2019: Fresh Start & High School Finale

Joined Penrith FC, reached grand finals but lost 1-0. Graduated and ended high school soccer journey.

2020-2023: Coaching & Development

Coached local teams, studied coaching & management. Led Blacktown Workers U16 Girls D1 to near-finals.

2024: The Comeback & New Challenges

Returned to play for St Marys FC, winning the grand final 3-0. Joined Nepean Ducks in gridiron as a wide receiver.
